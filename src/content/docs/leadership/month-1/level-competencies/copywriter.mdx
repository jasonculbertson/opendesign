# Copywriter Competencies

<div class="gradient-panel">
  <p class="text-[17px] leading-7 text-[#1a1f36]">
    These competencies outline the expectations and growth path for Copywriters at each level. Use this framework to understand your current level and what's needed to advance to the next level.
  </p>
</div>

## Jr Copywriter (L1)

<div class="competencies-table">
<table>
<thead>
<tr>
<th style="width: 150px;">Area</th>
<th>Competencies</th>
</tr>
</thead>
<tbody>
<tr>
<td>Skill (Individual)</td>
<td>• Developing an understanding of copywriting fundamentals and beginning to apply these skills to small to medium-sized marketing projects<br />• Crafting precise, error-free content for marketing projects<br />• Beginning to create copy that reflects our brand and clearly communicates our story</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Influence (Team)</td>
<td>• Developing your ability to present your copy decisions clearly<br />• Beginning to influence decision-making for marketing projects among your core team<br />• Supportive and engaged with the marketing team and culture</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Thinking (Team)</td>
<td>• Spending time fully understanding project needs and partnering with your manager to coordinate and organize assigned projects<br />• Starting to propose improvements to existing processes<br />• Contributing to project planning and coordination<br />• Learning to create clear timelines and ownership responsibilities and communicating clearly with project partners</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>
</div>

## Copywriter (L2)

<div class="competencies-table">
<table>
<thead>
<tr>
<th style="width: 150px;">Area</th>
<th>Competencies</th>
</tr>
</thead>
<tbody>
<tr>
<td>Skill (Individual)</td>
<td>• Solid understanding of core copywriting skills and confidently applying these skills to small to medium-sized marketing projects<br />• Creating copy that reflects our brand and clearly communicates our story</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Influence (Team)</td>
<td>• Clearly and effectively presenting your copy decisions<br />• Beginning to build compelling arguments that consider brand, business, and organizational needs<br />• Influencing decisions among your core team and beginning to drive broader stakeholder alignment</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Thinking (Team)</td>
<td>• Spending time fully understanding project needs and contributing to the project planning process<br />• Proposing improvements to existing processes, prioritizing your work, and tracking project goals and progress<br />• Leading steps in the project planning process, such as ownership assignments, crafting budgets, and tracking deliverables</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>
</div>

## Sr Copywriter (L3)

<div class="competencies-table">
<table>
<thead>
<tr>
<th style="width: 150px;">Area</th>
<th>Competencies</th>
</tr>
</thead>
<tbody>
<tr>
<td>Skill (Individual)</td>
<td>• Strong understanding of core copywriting skills and confidently applying these skills to large marketing projects<br />• Consistently writing copy that reflects our brand and clearly communicates our story<br />• Beginning to concept new copy directions and overarching narratives for projects</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Influence (Team)</td>
<td>• Proactively developing the approach, project stages, processes, and timeline to deliver on brand design projects<br />• Effectively presenting copy decisions and building compelling arguments<br />• Proactively influencing decisions and working with leaders from other teams to drive broader alignment</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Thinking (Team)</td>
<td>• Spending time fully understanding organizational needs and establishing clear processes with multiple stakeholders or teams<br />• Prioritizing work effectively based on business and customer needs and documenting your thinking with minimal guidance<br />• Comfortable working with project tools to document your work and defining new tools, templates, or systems to recommend better ways of working</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>
</div>

## Copy Director (L4)

<div class="competencies-table">
<table>
<thead>
<tr>
<th style="width: 150px;">Area</th>
<th>Competencies</th>
</tr>
</thead>
<tbody>
<tr>
<td>Skill (Individual)</td>
<td>• Expert understanding of core copywriting skills and investing in improving the quality of overall brand systems<br />• Confidently applying expertise to extra-large marketing projects<br />• Consistently writing copy that reflects our brand and clearly communicates our story<br />• Proactively concepting new visual directions and overarching narratives for projects</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Influence (Team)</td>
<td>• Clearly providing direction to others to execute your vision<br />• Presenting copy decisions expertly and building compelling arguments<br />• Proactively working with leaders from other teams to drive alignment<br />• Teaching others the art of storytelling, influence, and presentation skills</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Thinking (Team)</td>
<td>• Proactively developing the approach, project stages, processes, brand research plans, and timeline to deliver on brand design projects and new opportunities<br />• Pushing the brief to new places at early stages of work and driving strategic ideation with cross-functional teams</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>
</div>
