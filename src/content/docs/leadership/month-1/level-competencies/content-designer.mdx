# Content Designer Competencies

<Panel>
  <p class="text-[17px] leading-7 text-[#1a1f36]">
    These competencies outline the expectations and growth path for content designers at each level. Use this framework to understand your current level and what's needed to advance to the next level.
  </p>
</Panel>

## Content Designer I (L1)

<div class="competencies-table">
<table>
<thead>
<tr>
<th style="width: 150px;">Area</th>
<th>Competencies</th>
</tr>
</thead>
<tbody>
<tr>
<td>Skill (Individual)</td>
<td>• Developing an understanding of UX writing fundamentals and beginning to apply this knowledge to content projects within your product area<br />• Crafting precise, error-free content<br />• Writing for end-to-end flows, aiding in interaction design decisions, and delivering content in partnership with Product Design</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Influence (Team)</td>
<td>• Collaborates with PMs, product designers, and managers, participating in existing processes<br />• Delivers UX writing projects that are small to medium in size (2-4 weeks long)<br />• Contributes to content strategy from user research through QA<br />• Begins to document work clearly for partners</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Thinking (Team)</td>
<td>• Developing an understanding of the strategy behind project work and its impact on customers and product goals<br />• Uses this understanding to start making informed UX writing decisions</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>
</div>

## Content Designer II (L2)

<div class="competencies-table">
<table>
<thead>
<tr>
<th style="width: 150px;">Area</th>
<th>Competencies</th>
</tr>
</thead>
<tbody>
<tr>
<td>Skill (Individual)</td>
<td>• Demonstrating solid understanding of UX writing fundamentals and confidently applying this knowledge to content projects within your product area<br />• Developing broader UX writing frameworks and playbooks for your pods or core product areas</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Influence (Team)</td>

<tr>
<td>Thinking (Team)</td>
<td>• Clearly understands the strategy behind project work and its impact on customers and product goals<br />• Proactively identifies ways UX writing can contribute to product goals</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>
</div>

## Sr. Content Designer (L3)

<div class="competencies-table">
<table>
<thead>
<tr>
<th style="width: 150px;">Area</th>
<th>Competencies</th>
</tr>
</thead>
<tbody>
<tr>
<td>Skill (Individual)</td>
<td>• Possesses a strong understanding of UX writing fundamentals<br />• Applies this knowledge across multiple product areas<br />• Leads UX writing work that impacts the entire pillar, either through expertise in a high-impact area or foundational systems work</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Influence (Team)</td>
<td>• Actively participates in the product development process<br />• Drives content work across multiple teams<br />• Delivers medium to large UX writing-led projects (quarter-long)<br />• Helps define new templates and processes for content documentation</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Thinking (Team)</td>
<td>• Clearly understands the strategy behind project work and its broader impact to the company<br />• Contributes to product strategy and roadmaps<br />• Proactively identifies UX writing needs across multiple projects and uses evidence to validate content decisions</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>
</div>

## Lead Content Designer (L4)

<div class="competencies-table">
<table>
<thead>
<tr>
<th style="width: 150px;">Area</th>
<th>Competencies</th>
</tr>
</thead>
<tbody>
<tr>
<td>Skill (Individual)</td>
<td>• Demonstrates expert-level understanding of UX writing fundamentals<br />• Beginning to coach others developing understanding of UX writing<br />• Leading UX writing work that influences the entire product organization</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Influence (Team)</td>
<td>• Plays a key role in setting product direction for the pillar<br />• Defines the business value of UX writing work<br />• Influences how content fits into the product development process<br />• Defines scope and prioritization for content-driven pillar work</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Thinking (Team)</td>
<td>• Deeply understands the pillar's problem spaces<br />• Establishes clear working processes across teams and stakeholders<br />• Autonomously delivers on large to extra-large UX writing-led projects (quarter-long and beyond)<br />• Leads strategic conversations at the pillar level</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>
</div>
