# Photographer Competencies

<div class="bg-[#f6f8fa] border border-[#e5e7eb] rounded-lg p-6 my-8">
  <p class="text-[17px] leading-7 text-[#1a1f36]">
    These competencies outline the expectations and growth path for photographers at each level. Use this framework to understand your current level and what's needed to advance to the next level.
  </p>
</div>

## Junior Photographer (P1)

### Photography Skills
- Strong technical foundation
- Creates basic photo assets
- Follows shot guidelines
- Basic lighting knowledge

### Technical Skills
- Proficiency with camera gear
- Basic editing skills
- Understanding of formats
- Knowledge of workflows

### Impact
- Delivers defined photo tasks
- Contributes to photo reviews
- Maintains style consistency
- Shows initiative in learning

### Collaboration
- Works effectively with team
- Communicates photo decisions
- Participates in reviews
- Seeks guidance appropriately

## Photographer (P2)

### Photography Skills
- Strong photography expertise
- Creates complex shoots
- Develops visual styles
- Advanced lighting skills

### Technical Skills
- Advanced gear proficiency
- Strong editing capabilities
- Manages photo libraries
- Production expertise

### Impact
- Handles complex projects
- Influences photo direction
- Proactively solves problems
- Mentors junior photographers

### Collaboration
- Partners across functions
- Leads photo presentations
- Provides constructive feedback
- Works autonomously

## Senior Photographer (P3)

### Photography Skills
- Expert in photography
- Creates innovative approaches
- Establishes photo standards
- Drives visual strategy

### Technical Skills
- Expert in photo tools
- Advanced production skills
- Develops photo systems
- Creates technical processes

### Impact
- Leads major initiatives
- Shapes visual strategy
- Mentors other photographers
- Influences team processes

### Collaboration
- Builds strong partnerships
- Leads photo programs
- Provides strategic direction
- Works with stakeholders

## Lead Photographer (P4)

### Photography Skills
- Recognized photo authority
- Innovates visual approaches
- Sets organizational standards
- Pioneers new methods

### Technical Skills
- Shapes photo infrastructure
- Leads system strategy
- Creates scalable frameworks
- Drives technical innovation

### Impact
- Leads org-level initiatives
- Shapes visual vision
- Develops photo leadership
- Establishes best practices

### Collaboration
- Influences organization-wide
- Leads cross-functional programs
- Provides executive guidance
- Builds photo culture

## Principal Photographer (P5)

### Photography Skills
- Industry-leading expertise
- Defines photo paradigms
- Sets vision for excellence
- Innovates photo practice

### Technical Skills
- Shapes technical strategy
- Architects photo platforms
- Creates industry standards
- Drives innovation

### Impact
- Drives company strategy
- Shapes long-term vision
- Develops future leaders
- Sets industry standards

### Collaboration
- Influences industry direction
- Leads organizational change
- Provides C-level guidance
- Builds creative culture
