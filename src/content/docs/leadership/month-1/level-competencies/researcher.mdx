# UX Researcher Competencies

<div class="gradient-panel">
  <p class="text-[17px] leading-7 text-[#1a1f36]">
    These competencies outline the expectations and growth path for UX Researchers at each level. Use this framework to understand your current level and what's needed to advance to the next level.
  </p>
</div>

## Researcher I (L1)

<div class="competencies-table">
<table>
<thead>
<tr>
<th style="width: 150px;">Area</th>
<th>Competencies</th>
</tr>
</thead>
<tbody>
<tr>
<td>Skill (Individual)</td>
<td>• Developing an understanding of core research methodologies (e.g., qualitative, quantitative, market research)<br />• Designing effective research with guidance from your manager and stakeholders</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Influence (Team)</td>
<td>• Creating clear research deliverables that identify relevant insights<br />• Proactively sharing findings with core stakeholders<br />• Clearly communicating processes and findings with support from your manager</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td>Thinking (Team)</td>
<td>• Developing actionable insights with coaching from your manager<br />• Ensuring research includes recommendations relevant to project outcome goals<br />• Receiving positive feedback from stakeholders on the actionability of your work</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>
</div>
