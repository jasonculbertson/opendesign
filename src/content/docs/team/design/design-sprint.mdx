## Notes on running a Design Sprint

<Panel>
  <ol>
    <li>Pre-Sprint
      <ul>
        <li>Define clear objectives and success metrics</li>
        <li>Choose the right team and stakeholders</li>
        <li>Prepare all necessary materials and tools</li>
        <li>Block calendars and secure a dedicated space</li>
      </ul>
    </li>
    <li>During Sprint
      <ul>
        <li>Follow the structured timeline</li>
        <li>Document all decisions and insights</li>
        <li>Keep energy levels high</li>
        <li>Stay focused on the target problem</li>
      </ul>
    </li>
    <li>Post-Sprint
      <ul>
        <li>Compile and share findings</li>
        <li>Plan next steps and implementation</li>
        <li>Schedule follow-up meetings</li>
        <li>Track progress against goals</li>
      </ul>
    </li>
  </ol>
</Panel>
