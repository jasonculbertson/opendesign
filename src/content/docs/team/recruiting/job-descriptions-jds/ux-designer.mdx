# UX Designer

We are seeking a talented UX Designer to join our team and help create exceptional user experiences for our products. As a UX Designer, you will work closely with product managers, engineers, and other designers to deliver intuitive and engaging solutions that meet user needs and business goals.

## About the Role

You will be responsible for driving the user experience of our products from conception to launch. Working with cross-functional teams, you'll help define product strategy, conduct user research, create wireframes and prototypes, and deliver high-fidelity designs.

## Key Responsibilities

- Lead the UX design process for assigned projects, from research and conceptualization through implementation
- Create user flows, wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity designs
- Conduct user research and usability testing to inform design decisions
- Collaborate with product managers to define requirements and success metrics
- Work closely with engineers to ensure high-quality implementation
- Present design work to stakeholders and incorporate feedback
- Help maintain and evolve our design system
- Mentor junior designers and contribute to team growth

## Requirements

- 5+ years of experience in UX design
- Strong portfolio demonstrating end-to-end design process
- Experience with modern design tools (Figma, Sketch, etc.)
- Excellent communication and presentation skills
- Experience conducting user research and usability testing
- Understanding of web and mobile design principles
- Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced environment
- Bachelor's degree in Design, HCI, or related field (or equivalent experience)

## Preferred Qualifications

- Experience in B2B or enterprise software
- Knowledge of HTML, CSS, and basic front-end development
- Experience with design systems
- Background in service design or systems thinking
- Experience mentoring other designers
- Contributions to design community through writing or speaking
