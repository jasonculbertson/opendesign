## Product Role JDs

- Product Designer: Lead product design efforts and create intuitive user experiences
- Content Designer: Craft clear, engaging copy for product interfaces and experiences
- UX Researcher: Gather insights to inform product decisions and user needs
- Design Ops: Enhance design workflows and manage team operations efficiently

## Creative Role JDs

- Graphic Designer: Design stunning visuals and branding materials
- Copywriter: Write compelling content to captivate and engage your audience
